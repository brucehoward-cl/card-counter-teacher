House Rules

Dealer hits on 16 stays on 17.

Dealer will not hit on soft seventeen.

No splits or doubling down is allowed.

If player busts, he loses the hand, but the dealer will still finish his hand.

If player gets 21, the dealer still finishes his hand, and if dealer gets 21 the hand is a push. Otherwise, player wins.

If both player and dealer bust, player loses.

If the end of the deck is encountered before the hand is completed, player will be prompted whether to continue.

How to Count Cards

  1. Assign a value to every card
  2. Keep a “Running Count” based off of the values of the card dealt

Hi-Lo System Card Values

  • 2 through 6 = +1
  • 7 through 9 = 0
  • 10 through Ace = -1
